Our Story

Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology, Delhi (IIIT-Delhi) welcomes you all to the 10th Convocation Virtual Ceremony, an ensemble of graduating students, family members, and the Institute community to share the enthusiasm, celebrate the accomplishments, academic excellence and technical virtuosity of our graduating students.

Our Vision

Our graduates who are entering a new phase of their lives are well prepared, equipped with the necessary knowledge and skills for their future endeavors, thanks to research-oriented courses, world-renowned faculty members, credit-bearing internships and field-study experiences.
