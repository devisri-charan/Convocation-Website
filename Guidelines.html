IMPORTANT GUIDELINES

I

 All those graduating students who have registered online to receive their degree in person in the convocation, it is compulsory for them to attend the rehearsal. Any student failing to attend the rehearsal as per the schedule given above, will not be allowed to receive his/ her degree in person on the date of convocation; no relaxation will be allowed under any circumstances.

II

 Parents/guests are not allowed to come to the Institute on the day of Rehearsal i.e. November 25, 2022 Further, Parents/guests of the graduating students (maximum 2 ) accompanying the graduating students on the day of Convocation i.e. November 26, 2022 will sit in C101 & C201, LHC and watch the function through live coverage.

No parents will be allowed in the Convocation Hall (C102), LHC.

III

 The program for rehearsal / convocation will remain unchanged even if November 25 and 26, 2022 are declared holidays.

IV

 Notwithstanding the above for reasons beyond control, if the date of convocation/ rehearsal is changed, the announcement will be made on the institute website.

V

 Children below 12 years will not be permitted.

VI

 All graduating students are advised to carefully note the convocation schedule given above. No request for change of date of convocation will be allowed under any circumstances.

VII

 For students not personally coming to attend the convocation the degree will not be given to his/her parents on the day of convocation.
For medal awardees, if any student not attending the convocation in person may authorize his/her parent to receive the medal on his/her behalf. In the letter of authorization the concerned student will have to provide the name, address and contact number of the parent so authorized to receive the medal. Further, it is mandatory for the parent concerned to attend the rehearsal before the main function.
For this they need to inform “admin-academic@iiitd.ac.in” in 15 days prior to the convocation.

Detailed Guidelines for conducting the Rehearsal proceedings

STUDENTS Rehearsal: on 25.11.2022 (Friday)


1

 All graduating students receiving degree in person are advised to report at 10.00 a.m. on 25.11.2022 on the Ground Floor, Lecture Hall Complex, for verification of physical attendance, Issue of Invitation Card and Lunch coupons to the students registered online to receive the degree in person. (Gowns will be issued on the day of Convocation, 26.11.2022). Entry to the Institute on the day of Convocation will be allowed only after showing the Invitation card. Only 2 guests and the graduating student will be allowed per card.

2

 For rehearsal student should occupy the seat in the Convocation Hall (C-102, First Floor, Lecture Hall Complex) as per seating plan which will be announced later.

3

 Students must occupy their allotted seats by 10.45 am. The Rehearsals will start at 11.00 a.m.

4

 During the rehearsal students should keep the mobile phone switched off and carefully listen to the instructions. Student will observe complete silence and keep sitting in the allotted seats.

5

 A briefing will be given about the procedure for distribution of degrees as well as protocol to be observed during the function.

Students are advised to remain standing at the following occasions:

6

 At the time when the members of the academic procession enter the hall and till they take their seats

7

 At the time when the Director makes the following announcement and reads the Citation:
“Let the candidates for the various degrees be presented.”

{All the candidates will rise in their seats and keep standing until the citation below is read by the Director}
“Further, by virtue of the authority vested in me as the Chairman of the Senate of the Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology, Delhi, I also do hereby admit the duly qualified candidates listed in the scroll for the respective Degrees and charge them to prove worthy of the respective Degrees and authorize them to wear the robes ordained as the insignia of the Degree.”

{All the candidates will take their seats after the above citation is read by the Director}

8

 At the time when the Director makes the following announcement:
“Let the Oath be administered.”

{ All the candidates will rise in their seats and keep standing till the oath is administered}.

9

 At the time of National Anthem

10

 At the time when the academic procession departs and leaves the hall.

IMPORTANT NOTES:

i

 Attendance at Rehearsal is compulsory. Those who fail to attend the rehearsal will not be allowed to attend the main function.

ii

 Parents (guests ) are not allowed to come to the Institute on the day of Rehearsal i.e. November 25, 2022

Convocation Ceremony on 26.11.2022 (Saturday)
Report at 8.30 a.m.

1

 Convocation Ceremony is split into two parts, Session I & Session II; the details will be announced during the Rehearsal.

2

 The graduating students will report at Ground Floor, Lecture Hall Complex at 8.30 a.m. to collect Academic gowns on returnable basis against security deposit of Rs.500/-. Those who fail to return the gowns will be liable to pay the cost of the gowns, which will be deducted from their institute security deposit while processing the refund.

3

 The graduating students who are present at the time of rehearsal will only be allowed to receive the degree in person.

4

 The seating plan indicating the order in which students and their parents will seat in the allotted hall will be announced later.

5

 For Convocation, students should occupy their seats in the allotted hall by 10.45 a.m.

6

 Students are also requested to guide their parents to occupy their seats in the allotted Hall by 10:30 a.m.

7

 During the function they must keep their mobile phone switched off.

8

 Student will strictly follow the procedure for distribution of degrees announced during the rehearsal as well as observe the protocol during the function.

9

 During the function they will keep sitting in their seats and will not move out of their seats.

Students will remain standing at the following occasions:

10

 At the time when the members of the academic procession enter the hall and till they take their seats

11

“Let the candidates for the various degrees be presented.”

{All the candidates will rise in their seats and keep standing until the citation below is read by the Director}
“Further, by virtue of the authority vested in me as the Chairman of the Senate of the Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology Delhi, I also do hereby admit the duly qualified candidates listed in the scroll for the respective Degrees and charge them to prove worthy of the respective Degrees and authorize them to wear the robes ordained as the insignia of the Degree.”

{All the candidates will take their seats after the above citation is read by the Director}

12

 At the time when the Director makes the following announcement:
“Let the Oath be administered.”

{All the candidates will rise in their seats and keep standing till the oath is administered}.

13

 At the time of National Anthem

14

 At the time when the academic procession departs and leaves the hall.

15

 After function is over and the convocation is declared closed, the choir moves towards the mike to sing National Anthem, simultaneously the entire audience in the hall will stand up and join the singing of National Anthem.

16

 The graduating students will receive their degrees as per schedule and announcements

17

 The students must return the gown to the counter failing which they will be liable to pay the cost of the gown.
